## Outsourcing

the state of affairs in which a <a href="https://essif-lab.github.io/framework/docs/terms/party" hovertext="Party: an Entity that sets its Objectives, maintains its Knowledge, and uses that Knowledge to pursue its Objectives in an autonomous (sovereign) manner. Humans and Organizations are the typical examples.">party</a> has an <a href="https://essif-lab.github.io/framework/docs/terms/objective" hovertext="Objective: Something toward which a Party (its Owner) directs effort (an aim, goal, or end of action).">objective</a> (better: an <a href="https://essif-lab.github.io/framework/docs/terms/expectation" hovertext="Expectation (of a Party): an Objective that is Owned by that Party for having a specific (set of) result(s) produced, where the actual production thereof is (going to be) outsourced to another Party.">expectation</a>) for the realization of a (set of) result(s), where the actual production of these results is expected to be done by a <a href="https://essif-lab.github.io/framework/docs/terms/party" hovertext="Party: an Entity that sets its Objectives, maintains its Knowledge, and uses that Knowledge to pursue its Objectives in an autonomous (sovereign) manner. Humans and Organizations are the typical examples.">party</a> other than itself.
